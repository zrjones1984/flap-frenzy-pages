Flap Frenzy is a fast, physics-driven arcade game from Buzzworthy Games. One tap guides your fly through an endless gauntlet of pipes across four worlds — simple to pick up, tough to put down.

Four Worlds to Fly Through

The scenery cycles as you climb — a cratered night sky, the Jungle, a Theme Park, and the Swamp — each with its own look and mood.

Six Power-Ups to Master

Endless levels that keep getting harder — every run is different.

Lodestone

Vacuums up coins and gems from afar.

Aegis

Blocks your next pipe hit, then breaks.

Second Wind

Saves you from a hit once your shields are already spent.

Phase Shift

An invisibility cloak that lets you pass straight through pipes.

Warp Core

Warp speed with an auto invisibility cloak — drag up and down to steer.

Autopilot

Hands-off guide mode — steers through every gate and grabs nearby pickups for you.
